About

Welcome to the flourishing world of Brandhope clothing inc. one-stop-shop from where you can browse splendid collection of corporate apparels. We are one of the preponderant screen printed T shirt manufacturers, presenting mens winter jackets. We present decent collection of men's wear including screen printed T shirt, sweatshirts and jackets, mens winter jackets etc., which all are carefully designed for giving the utmost level of comfort. Made from top quality raw material, our mens winter jackets and sweatshirts and jackets are universally appreciated for their reliability.

With the main aim of providing superior quality men's wear. Brandhope clothing inc. was incorporated in the year 2012. We are a noted manufacturer & supplier of all kinds of brand builder gift items, corporate gifts, promotional gifts etc., since 4 years.We bring wide assortments of impressive range of products includes T-shirts, caps, bags, jackets, sweatshirts, bean bags, gift novelty items etc. We are a quality-centric business house which is devotedly engaged in the manufacturing of an exclusive range of men's wear ranging from T shirt, sweatshirts and jackets, mens winter jackets etc., and also bring brand builder gift items, corporate gifts and promotional gifts.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Brandhope clothing inc. is a Vertically Integrated Company, all the processes starting from yarn, Fabric ,Cut , Sew operations , Finishing & Packing are managed in-house which ensures a complete quality control. The Company, its facilities, manufacturing processes, work environment. Brandhope's infrastructure includes the following modern equipment and capabilities:

Fabric

In house Knitted Fabric - high quality fabrics including single and double jersey, interlocks , pique ,fleece, Viscose lycra, jacquards, auto/engineered stripes, yarn dyed. Equipped with capacity of 100 tons.

Stitching

High Speed Sewing - Precision cutting and sewing machines ensure a perfect fit to our garments.Apart from the machineries available, the Co's has an adequate skilled labour force in stitching unit. The total production capacity of all the type of garments is around 800 pieces per day.
